# Plan 6.2: Scaling Down 'Why Choose Us' Cards

## Objective
Scale down and optimize the "Why Choose Us" benefit cards to make them sleek, precise, and professional, removing the "oversized and bloated" appearance.

## Context
- .gsd/SPEC.md
- App.tsx (WhyChooseUs component)

## Tasks

<task type="auto">
  <name>Redesign Benefit Cards Scale & Grid Layout</name>
  <files>App.tsx</files>
  <action>
    - Change the features grid layout in `WhyChooseUs` from `grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2` to `grid-cols-1 sm:grid-cols-2 lg:grid-cols-4` (placing all 4 cards in a single row on large screens).
    - Tighten the grid gap from `gap-12` to `gap-6 lg:gap-8`.
    - Reduce card padding from `p-12` (very bloated) to `p-6 md:p-8`.
    - Reduce card offset shadow from `shadow-[12px_12px_0px_0px_#000]` to `shadow-[8px_8px_0px_0px_#000]` (hover shadow to `12px`).
  </action>
  <verify>Visually check the cards on both desktop and mobile layouts.</verify>
  <done>Cards are displayed in a clean 4-column layout on desktop, looking extremely precise.</done>
</task>

<task type="auto">
  <name>Scale Down Card Content Elements</name>
  <files>App.tsx</files>
  <action>
    - Scale down the icon container from `w-20 h-20 bg-[#00ff88]` to `w-14 h-14 bg-[#00ff88]`.
    - Scale down the Lucide icon `size` from `40` to `28` inside the container.
    - Reduce the title font size from `text-4xl` to `text-2xl` or `text-3xl`.
    - Reduce the description text from `text-xl` to `text-base` or `text-sm`, and change from uppercase to standard capitalization if needed, or keep it capitalized but compact.
  </action>
  <verify>Ensure text reads beautifully and does not wrap awkwardly.</verify>
  <done>All internal card contents are scaled perfectly to fit the smaller layout.</done>
</task>

## Success Criteria
- [ ] Why Choose Us card width and height are scaled down by ~40% on desktop.
- [ ] All 4 benefits are aligned in a single row on desktop views.
- [ ] Card text and icons are clean, high-precision, and professional.
