# Plan 5.1: Scaling & Spacing Refinement

## Objective
Scale down oversized components and standardize section spacing to move from a "zoomed-in" look to a precise, high-end Neo-Brutalist aesthetic.

## Context
- .gsd/SPEC.md
- App.tsx
- components/SectionHeader.tsx

## Tasks

<task type="auto">
  <name>Scale Down Hero & Decorative Text</name>
  <files>App.tsx</files>
  <action>
    - Reduce Hero section min-height from `min-h-screen` to `min-h-[85vh]` or `min-h-[80vh]`.
    - Scale down the large decorative background text from `text-[20rem]` to `text-[12rem]`.
    - Adjust the main Hero box padding and h1 font size slightly (e.g., `lg:text-[8vw]` to `lg:text-[7vw]`) to prevent a "stuffed" feeling.
    - Reduce the decorative text rotation/translation slightly to keep it contained.
  </action>
  <verify>Visually check the Hero section height and text sizing in the browser.</verify>
  <done>Hero section fits better on screen without feeling overly zoomed or clipped.</done>
</task>

<task type="auto">
  <name>Standardize Section Spacing & Grid Scale</name>
  <files>App.tsx, components/SectionHeader.tsx</files>
  <action>
    - Global replacement of `py-32` with `py-24` or `py-20` in `App.tsx` sections to reduce excessive vertical space.
    - In `SectionHeader.tsx`, reduce `mb-32` to `mb-20` or `mb-16`.
    - Update the featured collections grid in `App.tsx` from `lg:grid-cols-3` to `lg:grid-cols-4` to scale down the product cards on large screens.
    - Adjust `gap-8` to `gap-6` in grids for a tighter, more precise look.
  </action>
  <verify>Check section spacing and grid layouts in the browser.</verify>
  <done>Components feel precisely placed and sections are more compact.</done>
</task>

## Success Criteria
- [ ] Hero section is no longer overwhelmingly large on high-res screens.
- [ ] Collections grid displays 4 items per row on desktop.
- [ ] Vertical spacing between sections is reduced by ~30%.
